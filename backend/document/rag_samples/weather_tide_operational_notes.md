# Weather and Tide Operational Notes for RAG

## Use in planning context
This note links environmental context to action prioritization.

## Practical heuristics
- Strong onshore wind and rising tide increase salinity pressure near intake points.
- When tide is near peak and salinity is rising, prefer simulated gate closure over pump start.
- During low tide recovery windows, wait-safe-window can be prioritized before restarting intake.

## Communication guidance
- Include expected reassessment interval in farmer advisories.
- Mark whether action is simulation-only in every notification.

## Risk framing
- Warning scenarios: communication + observation-focused steps are often sufficient.
- Critical scenarios: combine communication with hydraulic mitigation simulation and HITL approval.
